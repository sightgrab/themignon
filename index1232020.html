The beginning

The founder Bhavna Agarwal comes from a house of industries, with a passion to work in the field of craftsmanship. To achieve her dreams she pursued her career in Leather Designing, from the renowned school of National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) graduating in 2016, taking along the titles of the “Most Innovative Use of Material” and “Best Graduation Project-II” awards. After gaining some experience she decided to move to the fashion capital of the world, Paris, where she accomplished her Masters degree in Luxury Brand Management from the esteemed university Istituto Marangoni and Manchester Metropolitan University. Her continued desire to become an entrepreneur led the road to MIGNON. Exploring the world inspired her in various aspects and leading to the birth of this brand “Mignon”, which is french for “Cute or Delicate”.
